Episode Description

In this deep dive into Ezekiel 1, we explore one of the most mysterious and awe-inspiring visions in all of Scripture—the wheels within wheels, the living creatures, the fire, the lightning, the throne, and the Man who sits upon it. What do these wheels full of eyes mean? Why does God reveal Himself this way to a broken, exiled people? And what does this vision teach us about Christ, His holiness, His Gospel, and His presence in our lives today?


Join us as we uncover:


The mobility of God’s glory in exile and suffering


How God’s all-seeing eyes bring both judgment and comfort


Why God reveals His terrifying holiness before He speaks mercy


How the rainbow around the throne proclaims the Gospel


How this entire vision points directly to Christ, the true Glory of God


What it means for the Church today to follow the God who moves



This episode is a journey into the fire and the thunder—a call to repentance, awe, and comfort in the God who sees, moves, and saves.
