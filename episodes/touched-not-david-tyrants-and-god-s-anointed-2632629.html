Episode Description

What do you do when the leader God put over you becomes a tyrant? What if he even tries to kill you? In this episode, we explore why David refused to kill Saul—even when he had every political and personal reason to. David knew Saul’s corruption, but he also knew something deeper: the kingdom of God does not advance by revolt, revenge, or the sword.


We dive deep into:


Why David refused to “touch the Lord’s anointed”


How resisting a ruler differs from overthrowing one


Why God, not man, removes tyrants


What Christians can do under unjust governments


How David’s suffering mirrors the kingship of Christ



Revolution is easy. Faithful patience is hard.

David shows us the way of Christ: the kingdom comes by promise and suffering, not by seizing power.
