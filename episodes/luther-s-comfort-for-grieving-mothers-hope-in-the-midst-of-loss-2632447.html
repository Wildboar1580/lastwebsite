Episode Description

In this episode, we read Martin Luther’s pastoral letter Comfort for Women Who Have Had a Miscarriage. Luther speaks directly to grieving parents, offering Christ-centered hope, tender counsel, and the reassurance of God’s mercy.


This episode is for anyone who has experienced the quiet ache of loss, for mothers and fathers seeking comfort, and for all Christians longing to see God’s compassion in the midst of sorrow.
