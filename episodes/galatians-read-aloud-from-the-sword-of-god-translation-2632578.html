Episode Description

This episode features a continuous reading of the Epistle to the Galatians from The Sword of God translation.


There is no commentary and no discussion—only the Word of God read aloud as it was given to be heard. Galatians confronts false gospels, exposes enslaving religion, and proclaims justification by faith in Christ alone with apostolic urgency and clarity.


Listen without rushing. Let the Law speak where it accuses, and let the Gospel speak where it gives life.
