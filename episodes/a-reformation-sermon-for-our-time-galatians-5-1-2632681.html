Episode Description

Reformation Today: Standing Faithfully in Our Time

This sermon reflects on the Reformation and its call to faithfulness in our world today. Grounded in Scripture and the Book of Concord, we examine how Christians are called to remain true to Christ, confess the Gospel boldly, and live as faithful strangers and pilgrims in these last days.
