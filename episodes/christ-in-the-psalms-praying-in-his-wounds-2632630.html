Episode Description

The Psalms are more than ancient poetry—they are the voice of Christ Himself. In this episode we uncover how every Psalm finds its fulfillment in Jesus, and how His prayers become ours through baptism, suffering, repentance, joy, and spiritual warfare.


We explore how:


Jesus is the true Singer of the Psalms


We learn to pray because Christ prays for us


Psalms shape our emotions, our endurance, and our faith


Psalms of lament, praise, confession, and victory become our own stories


Our suffering is carried inside the prayers of Christ Himself



We walk through key Psalms—22, 23, 51, and 2—to see how Christ lives, dies, rises, and reigns in their verses. When we pray the Psalms, we are praying inside His wounds, His victory, and His everlasting kingship.


🎙 Come discover how the Psalms are the prayer book of Jesus… and the living voice of His Church.
