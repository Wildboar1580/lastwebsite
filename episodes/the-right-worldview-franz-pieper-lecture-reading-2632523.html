Episode Description

What is the true Christian worldview—and how does it stand against the spirit of the age?


In this episode of The Last Christian Church on Earth, we present a reading of Franz Pieper’s powerful lecture The Right Worldview, originally delivered to the Synod of Delegates in 1923. Pieper cuts through modern confusion with clarity drawn from Holy Scripture, confessing Christ as the center of all truth, reason, and meaning.


This is not motivational fluff.

This is theology with a backbone.


If you’re weary of shallow Christianity, cultural compromise, and false unity, Pieper’s words still strike with prophetic force.
