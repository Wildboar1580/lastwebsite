Episode Description

This episode calls the Lutheran Church—Missouri Synod back to the teaching of C.F.W. Walther’s Church and Ministry. We expose how the LCMS “reconciliation process” replaces repentance with bureaucracy and how true unity can only be found through the pure Word and faithful discipline. Includes readings from Walther’s Theses on Church and Ministry.
