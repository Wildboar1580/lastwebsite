Episode Description

Discover The Sword of God, a bold, faithful English translation designed to be heard as Scripture was meant to be—alive, sharp, and uncompromising. In this episode, we introduce the translation’s method, textual basis, and guiding convictions, and explain how upcoming readings of the Epistle to the Galatians will allow God’s Word to speak directly to your heart. Perfect for daily listening, study, and reflection.
