Episode Description

This episode features a complete reading

of the Epistle to Philemon

from the God’s Word® Translation (GW) of the Holy Scriptures.


Philemon is Paul’s personal appeal on behalf of Onesimus,

where Christian freedom, authority, forgiveness,

and love in Christ are brought together in a single letter.


This reading is presented without commentary,

for hearing and meditation.
