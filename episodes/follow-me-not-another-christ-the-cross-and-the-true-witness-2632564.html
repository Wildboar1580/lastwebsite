Episode Description

The risen Christ restores Peter, redirects comparison, and anchors discipleship in obedience to His personal call. In this doctrinally intense, Luther-style proclamation, we explore vocation without rivalry, martyrdom as hidden glory, eschatology without rumor, and the unshakable certainty of apostolic testimony. Christ does not call us into someone else’s path, but into faithful surrender to His — the Crucified and Living Lord who alone commands: Follow Me.
