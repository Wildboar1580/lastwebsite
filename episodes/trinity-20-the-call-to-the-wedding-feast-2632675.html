Episode Description

In this sermon for Trinity 20, The Last Christian Church on Earth proclaims Christ’s invitation to the great wedding feast of the Lamb. The Gospel calls not only the outwardly religious but also the weary, the wandering, and the forgotten—to be clothed with the righteousness of Christ alone. Yet many reject the invitation, choosing the garments of their own works or the distractions of the world.

Here we confront the danger of indifference, the deceit of self-righteousness, and the mercy of God who still sends His servants into the highways and hedges to compel sinners to come in.


Rooted in Scripture and the Confessions, this sermon calls the hearer to repentance, faith, and holy living as those who have been found and clothed by grace.
