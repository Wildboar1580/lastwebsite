Episode Description

This week we’re talking about a topic most men overlook, yet it shapes how they carry themselves in work, family life, leadership, and faith: how a Christian man dresses. We’ll explore what Scripture reveals about the way men present themselves, why appearance mattered from Adam onward, and what the Bible says a believer should actually put on every day.


We’ll talk about confidence, dignity, discipline, and how being properly prepared each morning affects marriage, fatherhood, career, and spiritual battles. We’ll draw from the Old and New Testaments, and even get insight from Luther and Walther on what makes a man’s appearance honorable.
