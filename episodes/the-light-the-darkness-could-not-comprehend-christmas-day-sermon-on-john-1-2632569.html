Episode Description

The Gospel of Christmas roars before the manger: In the beginning was the Word.


This Christmas Day sermon proclaims Christ not as sentiment, but as Creator clothed in flesh, Light invading darkness, and Grace poured out for sinners.

Delivered in rhythmic cadence in the spirit of Luther, the sermon announces the eternal Word made visible, tangible, and given for you.


At the dining table, yet in the Divine Service—Christ comes where His Word is heard.


Here the mystery stands unshaken: the only-begotten Son, in the Father’s bosom, has declared God to us.

Hear the Light. Receive the Word. Depart in His peace.
