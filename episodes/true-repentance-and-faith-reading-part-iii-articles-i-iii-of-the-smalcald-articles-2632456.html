Episode Description

In this episode, we read Part III, Articles I through III of the Smalcald Articles by Martin Luther. These articles confront the heart with the reality of sin, the nature of true repentance, and the comforting promise of Christ’s righteousness.


Luther teaches us that repentance is not about human effort, but about the Law exposing sin and the Gospel creating faith in Christ alone. This reading is meant to strengthen your conscience, deepen your understanding of God’s mercy, and guide you to a living faith.
