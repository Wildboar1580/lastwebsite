Episode Description

In this first episode, Pastor Charles Wiese introduces The Last Christian Church on Earth—a confessional Lutheran ministry calling believers back to the pure Gospel, repentance, and faithfulness to Christ’s Word. Learn the story behind the name and what to expect in future episodes of preaching, teaching, and encouragement for those who still hold fast to the truth in these last days.
