Episode Description

This episode features a complete reading of the Epistle to Philemon

from The Sword of God translation of the Holy Scriptures.


Philemon is the shortest of Paul’s letters, yet among the most demanding.

Here the apostle does not command, but appeals — binding conscience, love,

and Christian freedom together in Christ.


This reading is presented without commentary,

for proclamation and hearing.
