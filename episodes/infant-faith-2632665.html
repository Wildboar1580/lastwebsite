Episode Description

Many Christians deny that infants can believe — but in doing so, they turn faith into a human work rather than a divine gift. In this episode of The Last Christian Church on Earth, we examine the Biblical and confessional proof that infants truly have faith.


From David’s trust at his mother’s breasts (Psalm 22), to John the Baptist leaping for joy in the womb (Luke 1), to Jesus’ own words that “these little ones believe in Me,” Scripture shows that faith is not a product of human reason, but the miraculous work of the Holy Spirit.


Drawing from the Augsburg Confession, Apology, Large Catechism, and Smalcald Articles, this episode shows that to deny infant faith is to deny grace itself — and to make faith into a work of man rather than the gift of God.


🎧 “The Faith of Infants: God’s Gift, Not Man’s Work” — because salvation is God’s work from beginning to end.
