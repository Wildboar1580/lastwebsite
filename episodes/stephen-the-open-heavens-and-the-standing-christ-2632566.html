Episode Description

The first martyr’s blood is not tragedy—it is testimony. In this rhythmic, Luther-forged sermon, Deacon Stephen preaches Christ enthroned above Temple, Law, and self-preservation. The world stops its ears and lifts stones; heaven opens and Christ stands to receive His witness. Hear the clash of Law and Gospel, the courage of confession, and the prayer of forgiveness that planted the seed of an apostle. Delivered with a sworded cadence by Charles Wiese.
