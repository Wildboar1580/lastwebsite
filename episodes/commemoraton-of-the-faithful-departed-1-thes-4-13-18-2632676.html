Episode Description

In this sermon for the Commemoration of the Faithful Departed, Pastor Charles Wiese of The Last Christian Church on Earth reflects on the hope and comfort we have in Christ amid death and loss. Drawing from Holy Scripture and the confessional Lutheran faith, this message reminds us that the Church on earth and the Church in heaven remain one in Christ—united by His cross, His Word, and His promise of the resurrection.


We remember the saints who have gone before us, not with despair, but with thanksgiving for their witness and with faith in the Lamb who has triumphed over death. This sermon calls all believers to live as pilgrims who look for the life of the world to come, trusting that those who die in the Lord are blessed and at rest from their labors.
