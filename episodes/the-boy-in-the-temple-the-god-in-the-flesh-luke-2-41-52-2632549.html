Episode Description

At twelve years old, Jesus is found where He must be: in His Father’s house, listening, teaching, obeying, and preparing for His mission to redeem the world. In this episode, we walk into the temple courts, hear the voice of the incarnate Word, and receive clear catechesis on the Second Commandment—learning to honor the Name of the Lord rightly in prayer, praise, and thanksgiving.


This sermon proclaims Christ as the obedient Son who fulfills the Law for us, the Teacher who answers every question of salvation, and the Savior who makes His people the true dwelling of God. Rooted in the Scriptures and faithful to the Lutheran Confessions, this message comforts the troubled, instructs the faithful, and exalts the Name above all names.


Jesus was never lost. He waited to be found. And He still does.
