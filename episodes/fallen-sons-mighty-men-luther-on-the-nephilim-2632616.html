Episode Description

Step into one of the strangest chapters of Scripture—Genesis 6. In this episode, we cut through modern myths and sensationalist nonsense to explain the Nephilim through the clear, sober lens of Dr. Martin Luther. Who were the “sons of God”? Why did the Church nearly disappear before the Flood? Why does this short passage provoke so much confusion?

Using Luther’s Genesis lectures, we explore how the line of Seth fell into apostasy, how the Nephilim became the violent “mighty men” of a corrupt age, and how God preserved the promise of Christ through Noah. This is not an episode about angels breeding hybrids—it’s about what happens when believers marry unbelievers, when the Church embraces the world, and when faith is despised. A doctrinal, pastoral, and Christ-centered look at one of the most misunderstood texts in the Bible.
