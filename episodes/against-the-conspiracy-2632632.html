Episode Description

Against the Conspiracy explores the biblical command not to listen to conspiracy theories and how they distract Christians from their true spiritual enemies. Many believe they are practicing “discernment” by hunting for hidden plots, but Scripture forbids this false wisdom:


> “Do not call conspiracy all that this people calls conspiracy, nor fear what they fear.” — Isaiah 8:12




This episode shows how conspiracy fear replaces the fear of God, how it moves the church to fight flesh and blood instead of principalities and powers (Ephesians 6:12), and how blaming human enemies can even recruit us into the camp of Gog and Magog, the final raging of the nations against Christ (Revelation 20:7–8).


Instead of panic, accusation, and worldly warfare, Christ calls His church to repentance, worship, prayer, love, and trust in His sovereign rule over every ruler on earth (Romans 13:1, Daniel 2:21).


The danger is not simply believing a wrong theory. The danger is believing that men control history instead of God.


Listen to learn how the saints wage the true war—not with fear, but with faith in the crucified and risen King who reigns over all.
