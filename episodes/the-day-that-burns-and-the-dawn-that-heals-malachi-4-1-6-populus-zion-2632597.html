Episode Description

Malachi’s final prophecy strikes with the force of a divine hammer. The Day of the Lord is coming—burning like a furnace for the arrogant and unrepentant, yet rising like a healing dawn for those who fear His Name. In this sermon for Populus Zion, we confront the reality of God’s judgment, the seriousness of sin, the necessity of repentance, and the comfort of Christ’s saving righteousness. The same Day that turns the wicked to ash lifts the faithful like calves released from their stalls.

Lift up your heads, People of Zion—your King is coming with fire and with healing.
