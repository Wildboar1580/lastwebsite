Episode Description

This episode is a watchman’s cry and a Redeemer’s answer. From Isaiah’s plea for heaven to tear open, to God’s revelation of hands stretched out toward rebels, the Word here exposes false hopes, unmasks self-made righteousness, and proclaims a Savior who descends to the unable and seeks the unseeking.


Hear God as Father, Christ as Redeemer, and mercy as God’s initiative—not man’s achievement. A message forged for weary exiles, wounded consciences, and stubborn hearts, preached in the Sword-of-God voice: sharp enough to pierce illusion, strong enough to break idols, and gentle enough to bind the broken.


For those who feel far from God, faint in prayer, or clothed in nothing but rags—listen close. Heaven has already been torn open in Christ. His hands were stretched for you. His peace is spoken over you.
