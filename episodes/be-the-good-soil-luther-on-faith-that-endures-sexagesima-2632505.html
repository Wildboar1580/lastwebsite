Episode Description

Why do so many hear God’s Word — and yet so few truly believe?


In this episode, we present a modernized, abridged sermon from Martin Luther’s House Postil for Sexagesima on Luke 8:4–15. Luther urges every hearer to examine himself: Which soil am I? Am I offended when the Word brings suffering, or do I cling to Christ and bear fruit in patience?


Christ says only one kind of soil truly receives the seed — and Luther shows why that shouldn’t scandalize us.
