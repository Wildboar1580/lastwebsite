Episode Description

Why does Jesus step into the waters of baptism?

What happens when the sinless Son of God stands with sinners?

And what does His baptism mean for you?


In this sermon for The Baptism of Our Lord, we hear the Gospel clearly proclaimed: Jesus enters the Jordan not because He needs repentance, but because we do. At the river, heaven is opened, the Spirit descends, and the Father declares His love—revealing who Jesus is and what He has come to do.


This message is about more than a moment in Jesus’ life. It is about God coming near, taking our place, and speaking a word of love over all who are baptized into Christ.


If you are weary, doubting, burdened by sin, or longing for assurance, this sermon is for you.


Key themes:

• Jesus standing with sinners

• Baptism as God’s promise

• The Father’s declared love

• Living daily from baptism

• Comfort for troubled consciences


“This is My beloved Son”—and in Christ, that word is spoken over you.
