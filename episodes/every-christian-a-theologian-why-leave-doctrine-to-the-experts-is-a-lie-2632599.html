Episode Description

This episode dismantles the modern myth that theology is for experts. We walk through Scripture, the Reformation, and church history to show that every Christian is called to be a theologian—and that you cannot judge truth without knowing God’s Word yourself. We look at biblical examples of experts who misled the people, the Bereans who tested Paul, and the dangers of turning theology into an academic exercise that tames God and silences Scripture. A call for Christians to take up their Bibles and reclaim the faith delivered to the saints.
