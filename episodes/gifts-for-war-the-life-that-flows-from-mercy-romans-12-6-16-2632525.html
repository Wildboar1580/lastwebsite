Episode Description

What does a mercied life actually look like?

In this episode of Outlaw Lutheran Podcast, we open Romans 12:6–16 and hear the apostle Paul forge the Church into something sharp, living, and disciplined. These are not soft words for comfortable Christians — they are battle commands for people made new by grace.


You’ll hear about:

• Gifts that are tools, not trophies

• Love without a mask

• Fire instead of fog

• Blessing your enemies

• Walking low instead of climbing high


If Christ has had mercy on you, then your life should look like mercy.
