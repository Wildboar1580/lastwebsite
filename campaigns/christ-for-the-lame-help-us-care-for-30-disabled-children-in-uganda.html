Christ for the Lame

Support care for disabled children and their families in Uganda.

Campaign Story

This campaign highlights the ministry's commitment to those whom the world too often neglects. Caring for disabled children is not an optional side project for Christians; it is part of learning from the least of these.

Support helps families carry burdens that are spiritual, physical, and financial. The campaign page gives donors a clear explanation of why this work matters and where to contribute.

Impact

  • Care and support for disabled children
  • Relief for parents and caregivers
  • Witness to the dignity of every person made by God

Goal

Care for 30 disabled children and support their households
