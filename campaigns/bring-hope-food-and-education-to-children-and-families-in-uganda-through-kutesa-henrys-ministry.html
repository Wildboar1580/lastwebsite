Uganda Relief Campaign

Bring Hope, Food, and Education

Support food, education, and Christian care for children and families in Uganda.

Campaign Story

This campaign gathers support for families who need both immediate help and longer-term stability. Food, schooling, and Christian care work together to strengthen households and communities.

The campaign page presents the need as a mission of mercy flowing from the Gospel, with a clear call to contribute through Zeffy.

Impact

  • Food support for vulnerable households
  • Educational encouragement for children
  • Partnership with local Christian ministry in Uganda

Goal

Provide hope, food, and educational support to families
