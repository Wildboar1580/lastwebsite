Uganda Relief Campaign

Feed 100 People in Uganda This Easter

This campaign provides Easter meals for Christians and families in Uganda.

Campaign Story

Easter proclaims the victory of Christ over death, and this campaign turns that confession into concrete mercy. Donations help provide food for Christians and families in Uganda so that the resurrection feast is marked by both proclamation and provision.

The page is designed to help supporters quickly understand the need, the deadline, and the path to give through Zeffy with confidence.

Impact

  • Meal support for families gathering at Easter
  • Relief for believers facing food insecurity
  • A clear and timely giving opportunity tied to the church year

Goal

Fund food support for 100 people during Easter
