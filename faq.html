Frequently Asked Questions

Questions about the ministry and giving

Quick answers for listeners, readers, and donors who want to understand what Last Christian Ministries does and how the Uganda campaigns work.

What is Last Christian Ministries?

Last Christian Ministries is a Confessional Lutheran ministry devoted to Holy Scripture, the Lutheran Confessions, preaching, theological reading, and practical mercy for Christians in Uganda.

How do I donate?

Each fundraiser links directly to a secure Zeffy donation page, and every active campaign page on the site includes its own call to give, timing details, and campaign summary.

What is on the podcast?

The podcast features sermons, Scripture readings, and readings from historic Lutheran theology, with a searchable archive that updates from the official RSS feed.
