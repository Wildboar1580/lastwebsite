Security & Trust

How to report an issue and how donations are handled

This page explains how to contact Last Christian Ministries about a security concern, what third-party services are used on the site, and what visitors should know before donating or submitting information.

Report a security issue

If you discover a vulnerability or suspicious behavior on this website, please email security@lastchristian.com.

Please include the page URL, what you observed, how to reproduce it, and any screenshots or technical details that may help confirm the issue. Good-faith reports are appreciated.

This website does not operate a bug bounty program, but responsible disclosure is welcomed.

Donation trust

Donations are processed through Zeffy campaign pages and Zeffy thermometer embeds. Last Christian Ministries does not directly process or store payment card data on this website.

If you are ever unsure whether a donation link is legitimate, use the official campaign links on this site or contact the ministry directly before giving.

Third-party services in use

This site uses third-party services for podcast hosting, donation processing, video embeds, and site delivery, including RSS.com, Zeffy, YouTube, and Cloudflare.

What to report

Please report issues such as suspicious redirects, broken form behavior, exposed information, malicious script injection, or anything that appears to compromise the integrity of the site.
