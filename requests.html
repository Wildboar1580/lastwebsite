Signed Requests for Help

These photos show widows, orphans, and other impoverished people in Uganda holding signed request forms asking Last Christian Ministries to help them. The forms are one part of how this need has been documented and brought before us.

The medical clinic campaign exists in this context. These are not abstract problems. They are real households asking for food, care, shelter, and mercy.
